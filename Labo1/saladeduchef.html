Salade du Chef

Ingredients

  • Leafy greens (e.g., lettuce, spinach)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. Wash and dry the leafy greens.
  2. Slice the cherry tomatoes in half.
  3. Peel and dice the cucumber.
  4. Thinly slice the red onion.
  5. Pit and slice the olives.
  6. Crumble the feta cheese.
  7. In a large bowl, combine the leafy greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, and feta cheese.
  8. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Toss everything together until well combined.

Serving

Serve the Salad du Chef as a refreshing and healthy side dish or as a light main course.
