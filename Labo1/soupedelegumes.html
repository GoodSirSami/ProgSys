Vegetable Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Celery
  • Tomatoes
  • Green beans
  • Peas
  • Vegetable broth
  • Herbs and spices (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Chop all the vegetables into small pieces.
  2. In a large pot, heat some oil and sauté the onions, carrots, celery, and potatoes until they start to soften.
  3. Add the tomatoes, green beans, peas, and vegetable broth to the pot.
  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with herbs and spices according to your taste.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
