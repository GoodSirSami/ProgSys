Escargots Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredients
Escargots
Butter
Garlic
Parsley
White wine
Sea salt
Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Clean the escargots thoroughly.
  3. In a small bowl, mix butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Place the escargots in a baking dish and top each with a small amount of the butter mixture.
  5. Pour white wine into the baking dish, enough to cover the bottom.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes, or until the butter is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
